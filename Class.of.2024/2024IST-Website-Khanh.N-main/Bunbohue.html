Bun Bo Hue Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef shank
  • 1 lb pork bones
  • 1 lb pork hock
  • 1 lb pork belly
  • 1 lb pork blood jelly (optional)
  • 1 onion, halved
  • 4-6 lemongrass stalks, bruised
  • 3-4 dried chili peppers
  • 2 tbsp shrimp paste
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb rice vermicelli noodles
  • Bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Cilantro
  • Lime wedges
  • Sliced jalapenos

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add beef shank, pork bones, pork hock, and pork belly. Cover with water and bring to a boil.
  2. While boiling, skim off any impurities that rise to the surface.
  3. Add onion, lemongrass, dried chili peppers, shrimp paste, fish sauce, sugar, vegetable oil, salt, and pepper to the pot.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 3-4 hours, until the meats are tender.
  5. Remove the meats from the pot and let them cool. Slice the beef shank, pork hock, and pork belly into thin pieces.
  6. Return the sliced meats to the pot.
  7. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  8. Assemble the bowl by placing a portion of rice vermicelli noodles in a bowl. Add the sliced meats and pour the hot broth over the top.
  9. Garnish with bean sprouts, Thai basil, cilantro, lime wedges, and sliced jalapenos.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy!
