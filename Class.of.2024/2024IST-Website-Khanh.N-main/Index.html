Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs (such as basil and cilantro), and meat (such as beef or chicken) . It is often served with bean sprouts, lime wedges, and chili peppers on the side. The dish is usually served with bean sprouts, fresh herbs.

11 hours

Banh mi is a Vietnamese sandwich that consists of a short baguette with thin, crisp crust and soft, airy texture . It is often split lengthwise and filled with savory ingredients like meat, pâté, pickled vegetables, cilantro leaves, and chili peppers .

25 mins

Banh xeo is a crispy, stuffed rice pancake in Vietnam . It is a savory fried pancake made of rice flour, water, and turmeric powder . The pancake is filled with pork, shrimp and bean sprouts . The crepe is served with fresh herbs and dipped in dipping sauce .

1hours

Spring rolls are a Vietnamese dish traditionally consisting of rice paper wrappers filled with vermicelli noodles, vegetables (such as carrots and cucumbers), herbs (such as mint and cilantro), and meat (such as shrimp or pork) . They are often served with peanut sauce or nuoc cham dipping sauce .

30 mins

Bun bo Hue is a spicy Vietnamese beef and pork noodle soup. It consists of thick noodles in a spicy hot broth, topped with beef slices, pork, and herbs. The broth is made by simmering beef bones (or sometimes pork bones) with lemongrass, fermented shrimp paste, sugar, and chili oil.

3hours 30mins

Bún chả is a Vietnamese dish of vermicelli, grilled pork rolls over charcoal, and a bowl of sweet and sour fish sauce., which is thought to have originated from Hanoi, Vietnam. Bún chả is served with grilled fatty pork (chả) over a plate of white rice noodles (bún) and herbs with a side dish of dipping sauce.

20 mins
