Spring Roll Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • Spring Roll Wrappers
  • 1 cup Cooked Shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup Rice Vermicelli, cooked and drained
  • 1 cup Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 1 cup Fresh Mint Leaves
  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce
  • 1 cup Julienned Carrots
  • 1 cup Julienned Cucumber
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Peanuts
  • Hoisin Sauce and/or Peanut Sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Fill a large bowl with warm water.
  2. Dip one spring roll wrapper into the water for a few seconds until it becomes soft and pliable.
  3. Place the softened wrapper on a clean surface.
  4. Layer a few shrimp, a handful of rice vermicelli, basil leaves, mint leaves, shredded lettuce, julienned carrots, and julienned cucumber on the lower third of the wrapper.
  5. Sprinkle some chopped peanuts on top.
  6. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then tightly roll up the wrapper, starting from the bottom.
  7. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  8. Serve the spring rolls with hoisin sauce and/or peanut sauce for dipping.
