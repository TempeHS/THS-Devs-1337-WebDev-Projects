Banh Xeo Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • For the Batter:
  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • For the Filling:
  • 1/2 pound pork belly, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • For the Dipping Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, thinly sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together rice flour, cornstarch, turmeric powder, and salt for the batter.
  2. Add coconut milk and water to the bowl and whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add a bit of oil.
  4. Add pork belly slices to the skillet and cook until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  5. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
  6. In the same skillet, add sliced onion and cook until softened.
  7. Pour a ladleful of the batter into the skillet, swirling it around to cover the bottom.
  8. Add some cooked pork belly, shrimp, and bean sprouts on one side of the crepe.
  9. Cover the skillet and cook for a few minutes until the crepe is crispy and golden brown.
  10. Fold the crepe in half and transfer to a serving plate.
  11. Repeat the process with the remaining batter and filling ingredients.
  12. For the dipping sauce, whisk together fish sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, water, minced garlic, and sliced red chili (if using).
  13. Serve the Bánh Xèo hot with the dipping sauce on the side.
