Bun Cha Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 200g pork belly, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 200g vermicelli noodles
  • 1 cup fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, Thai basil)
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 cup pickled carrots and daikon
  • Chili peppers, thinly sliced (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, combine the ground pork, fish sauce, soy sauce, honey or sugar, minced garlic, black pepper, and salt. Mix well and let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the vinegar and lime juice. Set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the sliced pork belly and cook until crispy and browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the marinated ground pork mixture. Cook until the pork is fully cooked and slightly caramelized.
  5. Cook the vermicelli noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  6. Assemble the bowls by placing a serving of vermicelli noodles in each bowl. Top with the cooked ground pork, crispy pork belly, fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and pickled carrots and daikon.
  7. Sprinkle with sliced chili peppers if desired. Serve with the vinegar and lime juice mixture on the side.
