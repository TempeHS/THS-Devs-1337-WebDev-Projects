Pho Recipe

Video

Ingredients

  • 8 cups beef broth
  • 1 lb beef (thinly sliced)
  • 1 package rice noodles
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2-inch piece ginger (sliced)
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 4 star anise
  • 3 cardamom pods
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Cilantro
  • Scallions (sliced)
  • Lime wedges
  • Chili peppers (sliced)

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, add beef broth, garlic, onion, ginger, cinnamon sticks, star anise, and cardamom pods. Bring to a boil and simmer for 30 minutes to develop flavors.
  2. While the broth is simmering, cook the rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. After simmering the broth, strain it to remove the solids and return the broth to the pot.
  4. Add fish sauce, soy sauce, and sugar to the broth. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. Bring the broth back to a simmer and add the thinly sliced beef. Cook for a few minutes until the beef is cooked through.
  6. To serve, divide the cooked rice noodles among bowls. Ladle the hot broth with beef over the noodles.
  7. Garnish with bean sprouts, Thai basil, cilantro, scallions, lime wedges, and sliced chili peppers.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
