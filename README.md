# A collection of [1337](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leet) year 9 'Designing for the modern web' Projects from [Tempe High School Designers & Developers](https://github.com/TempeHS)!
This task requires students to design and develop a responsive website that supports young people understand or navigate an issue (personal or social justice) they face. Students use HTML/CSS/JS with a specific focus on the [Bootstrap UX/UI libarry](https://getbootstrap.com/) developing the site in the text based intergrated developer environment [VSCode](https://code.visualstudio.com/).
